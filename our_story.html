Home is where the heart is. We believe that if you can create a paradise at home, you can create a paradise in life.



It started with an idea in a messy, disorganized cubicle...



“How great would it be to have a one-stop shop for your home needs and living essentials?” We recognized that we have ideas that people can use, so we put our problem-solving hats on and got to work. Thus, Simplified Home Solutions was born.



The SHS Mission



Simplified Home Solutions strives to become the world’s leading providers of easy home living. Using our portfolio of unique consumer products, we seek to make shopping more convenient to allow time for what matters most.

From home to kitchen to health and everything else in between, we want to create a place where people can find creative and practical solutions that complement their ideal lifestyles.



Enough about us — let’s talk about you.



Simplified Home Solutions wants to give you the ease of shopping for your home goods from the comfort of your home. People are busy, we understand. Sometimes there just aren’t enough hours in the day. It can be stressful trying to balance what we need to do and what we want to do, so our team of designers keep you in mind and take care of all household lifting.

Our time, our solutions, your home.
